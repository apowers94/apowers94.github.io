FAQ

What is Docmarket?

Docmarket is a study engine that transforms your notes into powerful study materials. You can use them for your own studying or publish them to the marketplace for other students to buy.

What kind of notes can I upload?

You can upload lecture notes, PDFs, study guides, or any class material you want to convert into structured study tools.

Can I use Docmarket just for studying?

Yes. You can keep your generated study materials private and use them as your personal study dashboard.

How do I make money?

After generating study materials, you can publish them to the Docmarket marketplace. When other students purchase them, you earn money.
